Privacy Policy

We do not collect any personal information about you unless you voluntarily provide it to us. You will only be required to give us personal information if you contact us through our website, where we will collect your name and email address. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Gas Share, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Gas Share may disclose your personal information, without notice, if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on the Gas Share app or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Gas Share; and/or (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Gas Share, or the public.

Automatically Collected Information

Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by Gas Share and this information site. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the josiahstephens.com website.
